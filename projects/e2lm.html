E2LM Competition (NeurIPS 2025)

M. Yagoubi, Y. Dahou, …, J. Zuo, et al.

arXiv, 2025 · NeurIPS 2025

Paper Website Cite

Summary

The E2LM Competition is a community challenge focused on early-stage training evaluation of language models, hosted at NeurIPS 2025 with a first prize of $6,000.

Highlights

  • Community-driven competition on predicting final model quality from early training signals.
  • Hosted at NeurIPS 2025 with $6,000 first prize and broad community participation.
  • Establishes benchmarks and evaluation protocols for early-stage LLM training assessment.
